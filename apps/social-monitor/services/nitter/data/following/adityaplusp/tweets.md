>

>  
> Instead of requiring users to prepay rent or worry about account cleanup, rent is just paid incrementally when accounts are accessed by the fee payer using the account. Simple.
>  
> So if an account is used once per epoch, it stays active and uncompressed without needing any extra maintenance. The rent is covered by whoever uses it.
>  
> For example, take a standard 168-byte token account that would normally require 0.02 SOL in rent. Under LSR, the expected per-epoch cost would be just 0.000005714 SOL, assuming rent prices stay constant.
>  
> And here's the cool part: as more accounts are compressed and the total state size drops, rent will actually get even cheaper over time.
