> 10 On-chain Trading Rules
>  
> Not advice, but writing this more as a reminder to myself after many painful losses, fumbles, roundtrips.
>  
> 1. Your no1 priority as a trader is to protect your capital, market might be hot doesn't mean you let yourself fomo-ing in plays that will rinse your money.
>  
> 2. Detach all your emotions out of your portfolio number, adjust your risk so that no loss should fuck up ur mental - don't oversize trying to " make it", slow and steady will win the race even tho that race might be boring but it will never zero you out.
>  
> 3. Trade the attention, when you see a bubble forming, don't hesitate to add fuel into fire, the early birds are the ones who get the worms . In trading, brutal truth is the crowd is your exit liquidity - not your ally, always sells when every1 fomo-ing at the top.
>  
> Crowded trade never plays out well
>  
> - example: during BNB euphoria, early bridgers printed while everyone else was still stuck on Sol. Narrative doesn't care about a few seconds delay in txs.
>  
> 4. Don't overtrade, the more u clicks, the less you think, as quality over quantity always.
>  
> 5. Laziness kills greatness, instead, spend time to observer everything, do DD on every runner u miss, and reflect on every trades you executed poorly.
>  
> 6. Cutting loss early, don't let the "BREAK EVEN" mindset to lock your capital, what goes down 30% can go down another 50%, and what already down 50% can go down another 80%.
>  
> 7. Focus on what you can control, accept the risk of nature of trading which is uncertainty, nobody can predict the next candle so learn to focus only ur entry, ur size, ur exit.
>  
> 8. Monitor everything, don't sit and do nothing
>  
> > Any vamps? (better ticker, image, or tech?)
> > Any valid FUD?
> > Any interesting wallets accumulating?
> > Howâ€™s the sentiment shifting?
>  
> 9. Stay humble in winning streaks, but confident in losing streaks. Pride. ego, and self-pitty are ur biggest enemies. The market humbles the proud, but it also never rewards the hopeless.
>  
> 10. Be comfortable sitting on their hands, for no trades is also a trade. Best traders i know only bid when good set-ups appear, and stay patient most of the time
>  
> Stay disciplined. Show up daily. The rest takes care of itself. Your success already written among the stars.
